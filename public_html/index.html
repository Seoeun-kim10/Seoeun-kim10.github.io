My Contests

Guri City Symphony Orchestra

I had the honor of performing Mendelssohn’s Violin Concerto in e-minor, Op. 64 (III. Andante-Allegretto non troppo) with the Guri City Symphony Orchestra during the 2020 Vision Concert II at KBS Art Hall on December 26, 2020. This memorable experience allowed me to express my passion for classical music and deepen my connection with both the orchestra and the audience.

New Asia Philharmonic Orchestra

In February 2020, I had the privilege of performing the first movement of Mozart's Violin Concerto No. 4 in D Major, K. 218 (Allegro) with the New Asia Philharmonic Orchestra at the Young Artist Concert, held at Youngsan Art Hall. This performance was a wonderful opportunity to showcase my interpretation of Mozart's dynamic and lively concerto.

Concerto Festival

On November 26, 2018, I had the privilege of performing at the Concerto Festival under the baton of maestro Stanislav Ushev, held at Youngsan Art Hall. During this remarkable event, I performed a selection of iconic violin works, including pieces by Mozart, Bach, Haydn, Tchaikovsky, Brahms, and more, with Pro Art Entertainment. This experience was instrumental in shaping my development as a violinist, allowing me to explore a diverse repertoire.

Stanto Philharmonia Newcomers' Concert

On January 10, 2020, I participated in the 2020 Stanto Philharmonia Newcomers' Concert, a special recital for students of violinist Minjung Seo, held at Youngsan Art Hall. This performance was part of my ongoing development as a violinist, providing me with valuable stage experience and an opportunity to collaborate with other talented musicians.

33rd Young Artist Concert - Youngsan Art Hall

On February 28, 2021, I had the opportunity to perform in the 33rd Young Artist Concert, organized by Music Education News at Youngsan Art Hall. This concert showcased emerging talents, and it was an enriching experience that allowed me to continue honing my skills and share my passion for music with a wider audience.

24th Kookmin Ilbo, Hansei University Music Competition

Family Week Fall Festival Concert

By: Seoeun Kim

October 30, 2024  Solo

On October 30, 2024, I had the wonderful opportunity to perform a violin solo at the Family Week Fall Festival concert. It was an incredibly special experience to share music with such a warm audience. I played Oblivion by Astor Piazzolla, arranged by Robert Longfield, letting the haunting, tango-infused melody fill the room with a blend of melancholy and passion. Following that, I delved into Themes from the New World Symphony by Antonín Dvořák, arranged by Lavender, channeling the sweeping, hopeful spirit of Dvořák's timeless work. It was a memorable evening, and I feel grateful to have shared these beautiful pieces with family and friends.

Largo, Bach Sonata No.3 & Wieniawski, Violin Concerto No.2 1st Mov

By: Seoeun Kim

January 14, 2025

This video features two contrasting pieces that highlight my current violin ability. The first excerpt is from Bach's Sonata No. 3 in C Major (Largo), a slow, expressive movement that emphasizes my control over phrasing and ability to produce a smooth, lyrical tone. The second excerpt is from Wieniawski's Violin Concerto No. 2 in D Minor, 1st Movement, a fast, technically demanding piece. It demonstrates my skill in executing rapid passages, intricate bowing, and maintaining precision at a quicker tempo. Together, these pieces showcase my versatility in both slow and fast tempos, as well as my technical ability.
